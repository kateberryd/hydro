Company Profile

Hydro Triciton is a clean Energy company that was founded in December 2019 with the purpose of bringing sustainable and reliable clean energy to Africa and other developing and emerging markets. The company has developed and owned a highly efficient (0ver 100%) hydrogen with which has been deployed to provide electricity to underserved populations.

Hydro Triciton Vision

1.3 billion people around the world live without access to electricity with 600 million of them living in Sub- Saharan Africa. The company’s vision is to develop highly sustainable clean, renewable energy projects in emerging markets and empower developing countries population with sustainable power.

Hydro Triciton is a winner of the 2019 Shell- All On clean energy challenge. Hydro Triciton has moved passed its initial idea phase in 2019 and has begun to generate revenues.

Hydro Triciton Management

Hydro Triciton Global is led by Choji Bare, an internationally renowned chemist and inventor for the highly efficient Hydro Triciton fuel cell energy system, together with a world class team of seasoned engineers, project developers, financiers and strategists. Mr. Choji is also co-founder of Elbare Research and Development, a renowned research outfit for product development leveraging on chemical technologies.

The Opportunity we saw

Hydro Triciton is addressing emerging markets- the fastest growing sector of the global renewable energy market. Mckinsey and Co. (Mckinsey and Co. “Darkest before Dawn,” 2012) estimates that there will be an additional 400 -600GW of installed renewable energy systems by 2020 and emerging markets will take the largest share of this market. This growth is driven by both the acute energy shortage and new innovations in energy storage systems and pv panels.

Hydro Triciton is in a prominent position to leverage its experience to successfully expand to additional markets in Africa as well as United states and Asia.

Some Facts About Us

Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide cost effective sustainable and reliable clean energy to households and SMEs in underserved regions in Sub Saharan Africa and beyond.

Core Capabilities

Hydro Triciton’s successful track record is based on the company’s wide range of capabilities and turnkey solutions for a utility scale fuel cell power system. These activities include:

Securing suitable land

ensuring safe and efficient connection to the national grid.

Carrying out all required permitting, studies and tests.

Negotiating and signing a binding long term PPA (Power Purchase Agreement) with the utility or government.
